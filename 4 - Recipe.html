Chocolate Cake

    Ingredients:
                    1 cup of oil (tea)
                    1 cup of milk (tea)
                    2 eggs
                    1 cup of chocolate powder (tea)
                    2 spoon of cake yeast (soup)

    
    Preparation Mode:
    
    Put on the liquidifier the oil, milk, eggs, chocolate powder and the sugar. Beat well until mixed.
    Add the cake yeast and mix lightly, doesn't need to beat.
    Put the mass in a greased and floured pan.
    For the 1st twenty minuts, do not open the oven.
    Once it's grown, insert a toothpick, if it comes out clean, it's good.
